Lab Mission

Welcome to the Fluid Dynamics Laboratory, where we innovate cutting-edge flow diagnostic technologies to tackle challenges in mechanical, aerospace, marine, and civil engineering. By combining advanced flow imaging techniques with micro- and nano-fabrication, we push the boundaries of experimental fluid mechanics. Our research spans incompressible and compressible flows, with applications in turbulent flows, drag reduction, flow control, and fluid-structure interactions, driving progress across diverse engineering fields.


Principal Investigator

Shabnam Mohammadshahi, Ph.D.

Dr. Shabnam Mohammadshahi is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at New Mexico State University. She holds dual Ph.D. degrees in Engineering and Applied Science from the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth (2024) and in Mechanical Engineering from Pusan National University (2022), and earned her M.Sc. (2017) and B.Sc. (2014) in Mechanical Engineering from Isfahan University of Technology. In 2024, she founded the Fluid Dynamics Lab at NMSU to advance experimental fluid mechanics and flow diagnostics, with emphasis on high-speed flows, vortex-dominated phenomena, and fluid–structure interaction.
